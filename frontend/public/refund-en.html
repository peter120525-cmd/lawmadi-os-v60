REFUND POLICY

Refund Policy

Information on Lawmadi OS credit purchases and refunds

1

Scope

This Refund Policy applies to the purchase and refund of credit products sold by Lawmadi OS (hereinafter referred to as the "Service").

  • Credits are a prepaid payment method used for legal Q&A within the Service.
  • 1 credit per standard query, 2 credits per expert answer
  • Payments are processed through Paddle (payment processor), which acts as the Merchant of Record and manages taxes and payments.
2

Credit Products

The Service offers the following credit products:

  • Starter - 20 credits / $1.50 USD
  • Standard - 100 credits / $4.99 USD (33% off)
  • Pro - 300 credits / $9.99 USD (56% off)

infoCredits never expire and are permanently added to your account.

3

Refund Conditions

Refunds for credit purchases are processed according to the following conditions:

  • Full refund: Within 7 days of purchase, if none of the purchased credits have been used
  • Partial refund: Within 7 days of purchase, if some credits have been used, the amount corresponding to unused credits will be refunded
  • No refund: After 7 days from the date of purchase

warningFree daily queries (2 per day) are not eligible for refund.

4

Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Send a refund request to choepeter@outlook.kr.
  • Please include the email address used for purchase and the date of payment.
  • Refund requests will be reviewed and processed within 3 business days of receipt.
  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method through Paddle.
5

Refund Exclusions

Refunds may be restricted in the following cases:

  • If usage has been restricted due to violation of the Terms of Service
  • If credits were obtained or used through fraudulent means
  • If credits were transferred to or traded with another person
  • If all purchased credits have been used
6

Service Failure Compensation

If credits were consumed due to a service failure but a proper response was not received, the credits will be restored.

  • Credits are automatically restored when no response is generated due to a system error.
  • If automatic restoration does not occur, please contact us by email and we will restore credits after verification.

infoWe continuously monitor service failure cases to improve service quality.

7

Contact

For refund and payment inquiries, please contact us at:

  • Email: choepeter@outlook.kr
  • Response time: within 1-3 business days
